I've put together the shared customer journeys for the April 16 product review. The goal: even though we're each building separate prototypes, we tell one cohesive story.

**What's in the folder:**

• `product-review-preread.md` — Pre-read for Max and Romain (narrative arc, metric definitions, open questions)
• `navigation-prototype.html` — Starter prototype with the shared navigation (Home · Inbox · Skills · Knowledge · Performance · Settings) and copilot panel. Built using the v8 UI patterns — use this as your starting point.

**Key things to keep consistent across prototypes:**
• Navigation: Home · Inbox · Skills · Knowledge · Performance · Settings
• Copilot on the left panel (creation mode, not assistant add-on)
• Metrics: Adherence, Success Rate, Coverage Rate, Customer Satisfaction (AI-derived from 100% of conversations)
• No shadow mode, no agent persona names, no form-based wizards
• The "frequency cancellation" thread is planted in Journey 2 and pays off in Journey 4 — this connects the whole story
